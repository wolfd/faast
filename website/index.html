Everything you love about hanging out with friends, none of the hassle of actually planning it.

 
 

Get Started

Social interaction, simplified.

Gone are the days of the Hey, what's up, want to hang out? texts that ultimately lead to nothing. Why waste your free time looking for friends who are available when you spend it with the ones you know are free? Figuring out plans doesn't have to be a pain. Whether searching for something to do right now or for later today, FriendCast does the hard work for you.

FriendCast works with Facebook to find out which of your friends are free at the same time you are. State when you're available, write a small note about what you feel like doing, and connect with the people you want to spend your time with. No complicated option menus, no unnecessary processes, no annoying ads; Just you, your friends, and time better spent together.
